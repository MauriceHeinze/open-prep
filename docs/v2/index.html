Do I need a paid AI subscription?

No. OpenPrep works with whatever AI tools you already have installed — free or paid. ChatGPT via Codex, Claude Code, OpenCode, or local Ollama models all work.

Is this an official TOEFL® product?

No. OpenPrep is an independent, open-source practice tool. TOEFL® is a registered trademark of ETS and not affiliated with this project.

Is it hard to set up?

Not at all. Download the macOS DMG, open the app, and click your installed AI tool. No config files, no terminal, no API keys.

Where are my essays stored?

On your Mac. OpenPrep saves every attempt locally so your practice history is always available, even offline.

Will it support IELTS® or Cambridge®?

Yes — the architecture is built for multiple exams and sections. Writing is the first focus; IELTS® and Cambridge® support is planned.
