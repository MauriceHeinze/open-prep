Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before downloading.

Is OpenPrep really free?

Yes. OpenPrep is MIT licensed and free forever. No subscription, no usage limits, no hidden costs.

Do I need a paid AI subscription?

No. It works with whatever AI tools you already have — ChatGPT via Codex, Claude Code, OpenCode, or local Ollama models.

Is this an official TOEFL® product?

No. OpenPrep is an independent open-source practice tool. TOEFL® is a registered trademark of ETS.

How hard is it to set up?

Very easy. Download the macOS DMG, open the app, and click your installed AI tool. No config files or API keys.

Where are my essays stored?

On your Mac. OpenPrep saves every attempt locally so your practice history is always available, even offline.

Will it support IELTS® or Cambridge®?

Yes — the architecture supports multiple exams and sections. Writing is the first focus; more exams are planned.
