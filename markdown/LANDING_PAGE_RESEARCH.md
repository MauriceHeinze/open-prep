# Landing Page Research — OpenPrep

*Answers collected 2026-06-19 for the marketing landing page redesign.*

---

## 1. User Persona
**Everyone preparing for TOEFL who already has an AI subscription** to Claude or ChatGPT.

- Age range: broad (students to professionals)
- Tech level: moderate — they know how to use AI tools
- English level: B1–C1, actively studying
- Motivation: study abroad, immigration, professional certification

---

## 2. Key Pain Point
**Too expensive.** Existing TOEFL writing prep (courses, platforms, tutors) costs too much.

---

## 3. Key Differentiator
**Free & open source.** No paywall, community-driven, MIT licensed.

---

## 4. Distribution / Discovery
- Word of mouth
- GitHub Pages
- TOEFL forums, Reddit, study groups

---

## 5. Landing Page Type
**Marketing website** — a standalone page (GitHub Pages) that drives downloads. Not an in-app screen.

---

## 6. Domain
**GitHub Pages** (e.g. `username.github.io/open-prep`)

---

## 7. Primary CTA
**Download macOS app** — optimize the page for getting the .dmg into users' hands.

---

## 8. Brand Voice
**Friendly & encouraging** — supportive, like a study buddy. Not aggressive or overly technical.

---

## 9. One Feature to Remember
**Works with your existing AI subscription.** Users don't need a new AI account or additional costs — they already have ChatGPT or Claude.

---

## 10. Competitor Pricing Context
**Varies by platform** — paid TOEFL prep tools typically use monthly or multi-month subscriptions. Verify current competitor pricing before making public claims.

---

## 11. Future Scope
**Yes, eventually.** Planning to expand to IELTS and Cambridge exams, but TOEFL Writing is the focus now.

---

## 12. Visual Assets
**Screenshots ready.** App screenshots are available to feature on the page.

---

## 13. Brand Identity
**Logo done, colors good.** Happy with the current purple (`#5e2f7d`) + green (`#30C08B`) scheme.

---

## 14. AI Branding
**Provider logos only.** Show the icons (ChatGPT, Claude, OpenCode, Ollama) but don't name specific models like GPT-4o or Claude 3.5.

---

## 15. Install Method
**DMG download.** Standard macOS `.dmg` installer is the primary distribution format.

---

## 16. Email Capture
**GitHub star as signup.** Don't collect emails — encourage starring the GitHub repo as the soft commitment / social proof action.

---

## 17. Main User Objection
**"Too technical to set up."** Users may be intimidated by:
- "Open source" = complicated
- "Local AI tools" = requires terminal/config
- The page must make setup feel simple and approachable

---

## 18. Competitors
- **Magoosh** (paid TOEFL prep)
- **Raw ChatGPT / Claude chats** (manual, unstructured)
- *(TestGlider, EdAgree also in the space but secondary)*

---

## 19. License
**MIT** — permissive, do whatever. No copyleft restrictions.

---

## 20. One-Sentence Pitch
> **Get structured TOEFL Writing feedback using the AI you already pay for — free, private, open source.**

---

## Summary Brief for Landing Page

| Aspect | Decision |
|---|---|
| **Audience** | TOEFL learners with existing ChatGPT/Claude subscriptions |
| **Tone** | Friendly, encouraging, supportive |

| **Future** | IELTS/Cambridge coming eventually |
