# Logo Brief

## Product Description

open-prep is a local-first macOS desktop app for standardized English exam preparation. The first version focuses on TOEFL®-style writing practice: learners choose a prompt, write an essay, submit it for AI-assisted evaluation, and receive structured rubric feedback with scores, explanations, and targeted improvement notes.

The broader product direction is a calm, private, academically credible prep platform for TOEFL®, IELTS, and Cambridge across writing, speaking, reading, and listening. It should feel focused, fast, trustworthy, and high quality rather than like a generic chatbot.

## Brand Feel

- Calm and direct
- Structured and readable
- Academic, credible, and modern
- Helpful without feeling playful or childish
- Local/private rather than cloud-first or social

## Current Theme Usage

- Use these exact colors:
- Primary accent for actions, active states, focus, and logo accents: `#c6005c`
- App background: `#f8f8f8`
- Surface/card/background white: `#ffffff`
- Main text: `#131313`
- Secondary surfaces: `#f4f4f5`
- Muted fill/backgrounds: `#f1f3f3`
- Borders/dividers: `#e3e7e8`
- Subdued text: `#67787c`
- Success: `#149545`
- Warning: `#f5a000`
- Error/destructive: `#e7000b`

The previous dedicated purple and green brand palette is no longer part of the design direction. The interface should stay mostly light, neutral, and restrained, with color used sparingly for focus, action, status, and evaluation feedback.

## Logo Direction

The logo should work well in a compact desktop app header and as an app icon. It should communicate preparation, progress, clarity, and trusted evaluation without leaning too heavily on generic AI symbols. Avoid overly playful study icons, graduation caps, chat bubbles, or loud ed-tech styling.
